EVERYTHING IS FREE.
Except for the time you spend.

Concept

A new concept of entertainment, working and learning the public place. You can spend here time with friends, co-work, or rent a room for a private event. What makes it special is the absence of regular food and beverage menu — visitors pay only for the time spent, while all other services are complimentary.

What Can You Do

Work

Surf on very high-speed WiFi, use office appliances, and enjoy the vibe of our creative environment.

Communicate

Gather at the table and share ideas; book a table, a room, or an entire FreeSpace.

Enjoy snacks and drinks

Drink unlimited amounts of coffee, tea, and fresh drinks; eat sweets and snacks for as much as you can.
